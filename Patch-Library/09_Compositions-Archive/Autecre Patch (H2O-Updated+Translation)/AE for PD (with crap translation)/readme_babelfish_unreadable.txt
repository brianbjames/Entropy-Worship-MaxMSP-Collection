The result of the work of sekvensorov leaves for the assumed time to corresponding Fm- synthesizers in the form of the commands of reproduction, values of the height of notes and cutoff frequencies of filters. Absolutely all sounds in the patch of autekrov are built on the frequency modulation and are generated in the real time.


Sonic synthesis occurs in the block [ simpleFM~ ], which presents two-operator Fm- synthesizer (bond of two sinusoidal generators, in also the control units of frequency and of the amplitude of the synthesized by them waves). The frequency of the first in the bond (modulating) oscillator Fm (modulating frequency) is assigned not directly, but through carrier frequency (Fc) and coefficient of harmony (H, harmonicity ratio):
Fm = Fc * H,
The carrier frequency (Fc, carrier frequency) assigns the fundamental tone, around which kolebletsya frequency (Fout) of the output signal:
Fout = Fc + Am,

Conclusion

Let us total of our journey inside the musical box of Britons.
After the intricate constructions of its managers of elements stands the sufficiently simple principle of sekvensirovaniya of Ryy; to each tool is assigned its metronome, which works at a constant frequency with the small deviations, proceeding under the action of other tools or random events. The amount of deviations in the majority of the cases is limited by such limits, that to the rumor practically it is not possible to grasp gallop or the breaks of general sequence, reducing the dramatic nature there is no work on.
After several minutes of hearing the music begins to overcome by its monotony, in spite of brilliance (you will focus attention on "magic" presety!), squeezed out of simple Fm- synthesizers. Variability it is sonic it is reached due to a constant (but periodic) change in the central frequency of tuned filter; however, the sense of the use of Navier-Stokes equations for this purpose is incomprehensible. The same effect it is possible to attain where by simpler way, after accumulating several sinusoids after adding a little noise.
The absence of melodic structures, large organizations of rhythmical groups, introduction, conclusion and culmination of work is an obvious drawback in the patch.
